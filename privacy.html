Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy outlines the standards, policies, and procedural layouts configured by Prabhu Mahat Chhetry ("we", "us", "our") regarding the processing, collection, safety custody, and eventual removal of personal information and log details generated when you access or interact with this web platform.

1. Collection of Actively Submitted Information

We receive and securely store any information you intentionally write, select, and submit through our contact portals. Specifically, this includes:

  • Your full name or organization name
  • Your email address
  • The category of your project request (Subject)
  • Detailed text descriptions of your technical requirements

This contact data is solely processed to respond to your professional inquiries, prepare custom software estimates, or coordinate technical consultations. We do not sell or lease this information to third-party list-brokers.

2. Server Log Records

Like almost all standard web hosting architectures, our server environment automatically records standard client parameters into secure log files. These files track:

  • Internet Protocol (IP) addresses
  • Browser application type and core engine versions
  • Referring/exit page channels
  • Date and time stamps of site navigation

These server logs are processed on a non-identifying basis to analyze web system health, calculate general traffic statistics, identify malicious connection blocks, and optimize localized search rankings.

3. Cookies and Advertisement Disclosures

Google and other third-party advertisement networks may serve contextual or personalized advertisements on this platform after standard validation approvals. These systems use tracking cookies to serve advertisements based on your previous digital journeys across this and other websites on the internet.

Users can select to disable tracking cookies entirely inside their individual browser configuration menus or manage their ad serving parameters by visiting Google's official privacy and ad settings pages.

4. Data Storage and Transmission Safeguards

We use industry-standard secure transfer protocols (specifically HTTPS encryption layers) to prevent cleartext interception of any contact messages. However, please be aware that no computerized storage system or internet-based data packet pipeline is 100% immune from decryption risks, and we cannot guarantee complete absolute safety.

5. Your Rights Regarding Your Data

If you have previously submitted contact requests on this portal, you have the right to request a digital transcription of the data we hold, or ask for the immediate and permanent deletion of your messages from our systems. To initiate this, please write directly to: prabhumahatchhetry@gmail.com.
