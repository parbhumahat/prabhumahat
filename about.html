Meet Prabhu Mahat Chhetry

Full Stack Developer, UI/UX Designer & Tech Lead

Hi, I am Prabhu Mahat Chhetry, a dedicated professional full-stack developer and digital solution creator. I build secure, high-performance web systems that integrate advanced data pipelines with clean, accessible customer interfaces.

My Career & Professional Background

Over several years of commercial and community-focused development, I have had the privilege to architect digital frameworks for local agencies, businesses, and non-profit associations. Notably, my work includes designing and deploying the streaming platform for Radio Sunwal and leading administrative portals for regional Leo Clubs.

I operate under a simple directive: web software should be rapid, accessible, secure, and straightforward to scale. To achieve this, I write optimized, semantic markup line-by-line, utilizing native browser APIs instead of heavy third-party bundles.

Core Competencies & Technical Capabilities

  • Frontend Development: High-end semantic HTML5, modern fluid layouts, CSS grid frameworks, responsive typography, and lightweight vanilla JS scripting.
  • Backend Architecture: Node.js server design, Express routers, session authorization mechanics, secure tokens, and microservice configurations.
  • Database Management: Relational schema blueprints, PostgreSQL query optimization, transactional indexing, and secure cloud storage.
  • Digital Optimization: Google Lighthouse benchmarking, Core Web Vitals optimization, organic schema markup, and AdSense compatibility setup.

Why Choose My Solutions?

I believe that web technology is not merely a digital brochure, but a tool for administrative automation and organic customer acquisition. I coordinate directly with my clients from the initial wireframe blueprint up to the launch verification process. My solutions achieve perfect loading benchmarks, ensuring reduced customer drop-off and maximum ad performance compliance.
